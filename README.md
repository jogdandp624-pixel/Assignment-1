# Assignment-1

Title

Design of Scientific Infographic Representing COVID-19 Infection Mechanism

 Objective
 
To convert a published research concept on COVID-19 infection and its effects on the human body into a clear and visually understandable infographic.

 Materials Required
 
Computer / Smartphone
Internet connection
Design tool (BioRender / Canva)
Research article (COVID-19 related)

 Procedure
 
Step 1: Selection of Research Topic
A research topic on COVID-19 was selected focusing on:
Mode of transmission
Infection mechanism
Symptoms and prevention

Step 2: Literature Review
Relevant information was collected from:
Research papers
Scientific articles
Trusted sources
Key points extracted:
Virus entry mechanism
Role of ACE2 receptors
Replication process
Effects on lungs

Step 3: Content Simplification
The collected data was simplified into:
Short bullet points
Stepwise processes
Key headings

Step 4: Designing the Infographic
Using BioRender:
A suitable template was selected
Title and sections were added:
Introduction
Entry into body
Infection process
Symptoms
Effects on body
Prevention
Scientific icons (virus, lungs, cells) were used
Arrows were added to represent flow of infection

Step 5: Visualization

Complex biological processes were converted into diagrams
Minimal text with maximum visual clarity was maintained
Color coding was used for better understanding

Step 6: Finalization

Layout was reviewed for clarity and accuracy
Infographic was exported in PNG/PDF format
